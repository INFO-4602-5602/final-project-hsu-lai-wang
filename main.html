Sentiment Trajectory

In the part, we try to explore the sentiment trajectory in reviews.
  1. Selecting reviews with more than 20 sentences
  2. Using logistic regression model to predict the positive probability of each sentence
  3. Interpolate probability vector to same dimension to build the sentiment trajectory vector
  4. Agglomerative Clustering all sentiment trajectories
  5. Plot the mean of clusters
